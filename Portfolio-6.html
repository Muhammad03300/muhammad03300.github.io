Delight Achar

Delight Achar is a feature-rich ecommerce website that I personally designed and developed, dedicated to selling homemade, organic pickles and chutneys. The platform offers an authentic user experience with a strong focus on tradition and taste.

The website includes:

  • Review & Rating System for customer feedback
  • Admin Portal to manage products, categories, and orders
  • Product Categories (Mango, Lemon, Garlic, Chutneys, and more)
  • Related Items feature for improved product discovery
  • Chutney Products along with Achars
  • Online Payment Gateway integration
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) option

This project not only showcases my frontend and backend development skills but also reflects my ability to build scalable, user-friendly ecommerce systems with real-world functionality.

About Delight Achar

Delight Achar is your one-stop shop for traditional, homemade pickles. Our mission is to revive age-old recipes and deliver authentic taste across the country.
